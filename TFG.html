The problem

The digitalization of all processes of a standard high school.

  • Paper as main support.

  • Handmade analyzing and data processing.

  • Slow communications.

  • A lot of handmade internal processes.

  • Study cases with low information.

  • Reactive management.

Our solution

SMS - A processes accelerator for educational centers

What is it?

  • Centralized system
  • Web-based, accesible
  • Modularizable
  • Thought to run in the cloud
  • Scalable
  • Open Source

What will it allow?

  • Minimize costs and management time
  • Profiles tracking
  • Help in decision making
  • Improve student performance
  • Early detection and preventive action
  • Reliable connection to other systems
  • Understand the state of the center

Based on what?

The digitization of processes and data as:
  • Attendance controls
  • Marks management
  • Attitude tracking
  • Event management
  • Disciplinary controls
  • Parental tracking
  • ...
